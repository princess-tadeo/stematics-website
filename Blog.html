Top 10 Uncommon Filipino Words in 2024

1. Lakambini - A poetic term for a woman of noble or regal bearing; a queen or princess.

2. Kaulayaw - A companion or partner, especially in romantic or intimate contexts.

3. Pangilakip - An attachment or accessory used to complement or enhance something, such as an additional item to a main gift.

4. Sukob - A superstition or belief that certain events or actions should not occur simultaneously to avoid bad luck or negative consequences.

5. Dapithapon - The time of day between late afternoon and early evening, usually characterized by the soft glow of the setting sun.

6. Pahimakas - The final or concluding part of something, often used to describe the last moments or farewell.

7. Barumbado - Rough or rowdy in behavior; someone who is aggressive or prone to causing trouble.

8. Talangka - A type of freshwater crab found in the Philippines, known for its sideways walking motion and aggressive behavior.

9. Balintataw - The pupil of the eye; also used figuratively to refer to insight or perception.

10. Sapantaha - A conjecture or speculative thought; a guess or assumption made without sufficient evidence.
